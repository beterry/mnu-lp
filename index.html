Our Story

Ben is a professional developer and Sophie, a professional designer. Together they had the skills, knowledge, and passion to create a solution to a problem they encountered many times while dining out.

In a pandemic era, many restaurants made an abrupt shift to QR-activated digital PDFs in place of traditional physical menus. As frequent restaurant-goers, Ben and Sophie began to notice a lack of well-designed, mobile-friendly menus. The digital PDFs and makeshift lists were not suited for small devices and required endless zooming to read. Using their talents and passion for solving problems through development and design, they began MNU.

“We hope we can provide a useful service for restaurants to be able to improve their customer experience, ensuring that they retain and expand their customer base.” - Ben
